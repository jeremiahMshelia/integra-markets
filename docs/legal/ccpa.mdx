This page is provided to satisfy disclosure and opt-out requirements
under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the
California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and equivalent provisions of the
Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), Colorado Privacy Act
(CPA), Connecticut Data Privacy Act (CTDPA), Utah Consumer Privacy Act
(UCPA), Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA), Oregon Consumer
Privacy Act, and Montana Consumer Data Privacy Act.

For the full description of how we collect and use your data, see the
[Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

## What We Do and Do Not Do

| | Status |
| --- | --- |
| Do we **sell** your personal information for monetary consideration? | **No** |
| Do we **share** your personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising? | **No** |
| Do we exchange your personal information for any other valuable consideration in a manner that would constitute a "sale" under CCPA § 1798.140(ad)? | **No** |

| Do we use your data to make automated decisions producing legal or similarly significant effects about you? | **No** (see [Privacy Policy § 2.4](/legal/privacy)) |

Because we do not sell or share your personal information, there is no
opt-out for you to exercise on this front. This page exists because
California, Colorado, Connecticut, and other states require us to
publish it regardless of whether we engage in those practices.

## Your Rights Under State Privacy Law

If you are a resident of California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut,
Utah, Texas, Oregon, Montana, Delaware, or another US state with a
comprehensive privacy law, you have the following rights:

| Right | What you can ask us to do |
| --- | --- |
| **Right to Know / Access** | Tell you what categories of personal information we have collected, the sources, the business purposes, and to whom we have disclosed it |
| **Right to Confirm Processing** | Confirm whether we are processing your personal information |
| **Right to Correct** | Correct inaccurate personal information |
| **Right to Delete** | Delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to exceptions |
| **Right to Data Portability** | Receive a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format |
| **Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing** | Direct us not to sell or share your personal information (already not done, as stated above) |
| **Right to Opt Out of Profiling** | Direct us not to use your personal information for profiling in furtherance of decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects (we do not do this) |
| **Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information** (California) | Direct us to use sensitive personal information only for permitted business purposes |
| **Right to Non-Discrimination** | Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights |
| **Right to Appeal** (Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Texas, Oregon, Montana) | Appeal our denial of a request |

## How to Exercise These Rights

**Email:** [contact@integramarkets.app](mailto:contact@integramarkets.app)

Include in your email:

1. Your name and the email address associated with your Integra Markets
   account
2. The state you reside in
3. The right you are exercising (for example, "Right to Delete" or
   "Right to Access")

**In-app self-service:** For Right to Access and Right to Delete, you
can use the in-app flow:

- **Right to Access:** Profile → Privacy → Download my data
- **Right to Delete:** Profile → Account → Delete account (see the
  step-by-step deletion schedule in [Privacy Policy § 3.4](/legal/privacy))

**Authorized agents.** You may use an authorized agent to submit a
request on your behalf. We will require written proof that the agent is
authorized and may require you to verify your identity directly with us.

## Verification

To prevent fraudulent requests, we will verify your identity by
confirming control of the email address on your Integra Markets account.
For sensitive requests we may ask you to provide additional information
already in our records.

## Response Timeline

We will acknowledge receipt of your request within **10 business days**
and respond substantively within **45 days**. If we require an extension
(allowed under California law for complex requests), we will notify you
and the response will be provided within an additional 45 days.

If we deny your request, we will explain why. Residents of states with a
Right to Appeal may appeal by replying to our denial; we will respond to
the appeal within **60 days**.

## Categories of Personal Information We Collect

In the preceding 12 months, we have collected the following categories
of personal information as defined under CCPA § 1798.140(v):

| CCPA category | What we collect |
| --- | --- |
| Identifiers | Email address, display name, federated ID (if you use Apple or Google Sign-In), hashed device identifier |
| Customer records | Subset of identifiers above |
| Internet or network activity | App usage events, sign-in events, source IP (truncated to /24 subnet after 90 days), User-Agent |
| Geolocation | Coarse geolocation inferred from IP only; no precise geolocation collected |
| Financial information | Stripe customer identifier and tokenized payment method (paid users only); the full payment card number is processed by Stripe and never touches our infrastructure |
| Inferences | Watched commodities, alert thresholds, content preferences |
| Sensitive personal information (CCPA § 1798.140(ae)) | Account credentials; financial-account identifiers (Stripe customer reference) |

We do not collect: biometric information, precise geolocation, racial or
ethnic origin, religious or philosophical beliefs, union membership,
genetic or health data, sex life or sexual orientation data,
communications content other than your direct support correspondence, or
information about persons under 18.
